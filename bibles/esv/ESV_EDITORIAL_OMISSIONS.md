# ESV Editorial Omissions (Intentional)

The following verses are **intentionally omitted** from the ESV translation due to editorial decisions by the Translation Oversight Committee. These verses are considered later additions to the manuscript tradition and are not in the earliest/most reliable Greek manuscripts.

**These verses should NOT be added back to esv.md:**

## Bracketed Passages (Textually Uncertain)

These passages are included in the ESV but marked as textually uncertain:
- Mark 16:9-20 (Resurrection of Jesus)
- John 7:53-8:11 (Woman caught in adultery)

## Parts of Verses Modified (Not Omitted)

Various verses where wording was changed for translation accuracy (not removed entirely).

## Key Principle

**If a verse number is missing from esv.md but is in this list above, it is INTENTIONALLY omitted by the ESV and should remain missing.**

**If a verse number is missing from esv.md and is NOT in this list, it is likely an extraction error and should be fetched from the API.**
